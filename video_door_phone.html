Video Door Phone

Overview

We have a wide range of high tech Video Door Phones that allows you to see and speak to a visitor before opening the door. You can choose between a range of Online or Offline Video Door Phones with a wide range of features and capabilities.
Online Video Door Phones: These are connected to the internet and enables you to see who is at your door from anywhere in the world. Few of these online Video Door Phones also have the capability of remotely unlocking your main door if you have the integrated Digital Door Locks.
Offline Video Door Phones: These are not connected to the internet but you can see and speak to the visitor at your door from anywhere inside the house with a wireless video remote. These models also have the capability of remotely unlocking your main door if you have the integrated Digital Door Locks.
