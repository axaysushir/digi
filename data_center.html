Data Centers

Modern data centre are built on an integrated architecture that focuses on optimal utilization of space, energy and component capacity. The solution allows single window monitoring and management of the complete data centre infrastructure including IT and non IT assets.

The solution provides best-in-class efficiency, agility, redundancy, availability, lower initial and operational costs by right-sizing equipment, maximizing use of available space and generating energy savings in turn providing best ROI.

Data Center Services - Digiteck Service not only designs, plans and constructs your data center, but trains your staff to maintain the environment thus reducing your dependability to the external team and optimizing the operational cost.

Planning- Digiteck Service works on a 5 year growth model for your company, understands your current and future data-processing needs and likely technological evolution. Based on this model, we provide a full evaluation report that includes electrical, mechanical and space requirements, along with equipment layout drawings, cost estimates and a proposed construction schedule.

Design- Digiteck Service ensures that the evaluation report shall act as the master document to generate a design for a data center. The evaluation report shall cover all topics like, power, cooling, fire protection, water leak detection, hot and cool aisle. Also, we can work with your architect, to ensure that unique data-center requirements, such as underfloor airflow for ventilation are met.

Construction- Digiteck Service can act as your project manager through all phases of construction. You get a turnkey solution no matter how complex the job is. Planning and design are available as separate packages, or you can give us the entire project.
